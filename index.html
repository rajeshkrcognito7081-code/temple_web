About Dharmaranya
Vedi Temple

✦

Dharmaranya Vedi Temple is a sacred pilgrimage site located near Vishnupad Temple in Bihar, India. Revered for its deep spiritual significance, the temple is believed to be a powerful center for ancestral rituals, meditation, and divine blessings.

Spiritual & Mythological Significance

According to ancient traditions from the era of the Mahabharata, King Yudhishthira performed sacred rituals — Pind Daan, Shraddha, and Yajna — at this holy site to free himself from the sins and karmic burdens of the great war.

It is also believed that Lord Krishna performed the powerful Tripindi Shraddha ritual here to neutralize the deadly Brahmastra and protect the unborn King Parikshit.

  • Peace for ancestors
  • Remove Pitra Dosha

  • Prosperity & blessings
  • Remove negative energy

Connection with Lord Buddha

According to Buddhist traditions, Gautama Buddha meditated in this sacred region during his spiritual journey before attaining enlightenment in Bodh Gaya.

It is believed that Queen Sujata offered him sacred Kheer after years of intense penance. Soon after leaving this place, Buddha attained enlightenment beneath the Bodhi Tree, becoming known to the world as "The Buddha."

A land touched by the Buddha's feet and blessed by his enlightenment journey.

ॐ

A Sacred Center of Faith

For centuries, Dharmaranya Vedi Temple has remained a symbol of faith, spiritual transformation, meditation, and divine grace.

Today, pilgrims and spiritual seekers from across India and around the world visit this holy site seeking peace, blessings, and spiritual upliftment.
